“A BRAND NEW way to CELEBRATE!”

Due to the pandemic,
most parents and families have given up on the idea of
celebrating their children’s birthdays.

Since 2020, it has been almost 2 years we haven't been able to celebrate our children's
birthdays the way we used to. Many have either skipped their gatherings entirely or had to
reduce their get-togethers to as many as is allowed by the authorities. Just as many and
even more are longing for a way to still be able to come together to share their
joy and connect like we used to.

Zoom's great, but based on what we've seen & experienced
with meetings and webinars so far,
it's just not the same as interacting face to face.

Sure Zoom is a great way for all of us to meet online, but all of us who have ever attended
a meeting or a webinar know that if it is just based off how things are done now, it lacks
the kind of interactivity that we all wish to have.

Perhaps, what we need is just some creativity,
innovation, a bit of time and a helluva
lot of effort to figure this out!

This is where Francis Ang comes in.
This is not your regular (let's admit - most of the time, boring)
Zoom meetings or webinars.

Determined to figure out a way to break down those walls that's in the way of all of us having a great time
interacting and connecting over Zoom, Francis set out to consult the industry's experts from all around the world.

Converting his home into a studio equipped with state-of-the-art broadcasting equipment, software, and innovative techniques,
Francis has skilfully blended technology with his Art of Magic to bring to you a highly interactive virtual magic show
- one unlike anything you've experienced before!

“ Presenting, a Brand New way
to celebrate your child's birthday! “

To Francis’ own delightful surprise, this new medium has both forced and provided him
an opportunity to create a brand new show unlike any other.
A show that involves magic specially designed to leverage on what used to be a major problem
- the fact that all of us are in our own homes.

About Francis

Francis Ang is an entrepreneur in the youth education industry as well as in the entertainment industry.

Throughout his career, he has inspired tens of thousands of students and fellow educators around the globe with his brand of highly fun and experiential learning workshops using magic as a tool to inspire students to fall in love with learning again and develop a whole new appreciation for math and science. He is also a professional magician with a true passion for the art of magic and has mesmerised audiences both young and old from all walks of life and all over the world.
